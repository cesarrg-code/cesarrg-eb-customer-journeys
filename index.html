Start with these five
elements

cesarrg.com
1 Moments
Registration
  • List the key steps of the customer experience.
  • Keep it simple.
  • 5-7 key moments is a good start. Evolve as you need.
cesarrg.com
2 Customer intention
Registration
Speed & convenience
  • For each step, write the key intention of the customer.
  • This is a reminder for driving the conversation around the customer's goal.
cesarrg.com
3 Touch-points
Registration
Speed & convenience
Web App
  • Touch-points are tool or mechanism the customer uses to solve their problem.
  • They can be direct interactions with people or technology features (emails, forms, etc...).
  • These are important clues for optimization.
cesarrg.com

Registration
Speed & convenience
